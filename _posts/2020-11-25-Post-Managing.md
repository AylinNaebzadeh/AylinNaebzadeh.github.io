First of all you must not be neglectful toward any lesson,all of your them are important.for achiving good marks you don't need to fallow some certain rules.Sufice to say we have two kinds of lessons in university.

1:) General ones: You need to know your masters very well.Recognize they take their exams in which level and what is their expectations from you ---->in this case you have to ask of seniors(students who are one year ahead of you).Befor your exams check the examinations which were taken last year.

2:) Professional ones: They are much more impretive than general lessons.In this part you must not just lean to your own masters'pamphlet.You need to study other teachers'leaflets from another universities such as Sharif,Tehran,Amirkabir,...
Even if your master was bad one(sorry:)) you should be your own teacher.

As a conclusion in university how to study your lessons is much more
significant than how to mange them.
