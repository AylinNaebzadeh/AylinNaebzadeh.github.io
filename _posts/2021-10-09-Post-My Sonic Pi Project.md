## My Sonic Pi Project

In this post I'm going to talk about my programming project.We have been told to make a song using Sonic Pi application .By using this app you can code your own song😃. This app has been made by Sam Aaron and suffice to say it's an inovative one.


To be honest my song is very simple.At first I chose my favourite song then I searched in google to find its chords .Just like python I put the chords in  3 lists(intro , verse , chorus).For playing the song I used of live_loops which cause your song to play forever.
Then for each list I wrote two live_loops : one for reading the list from the first element([0]) to the last one and second one for performing the chords. I decided to perform whole of my chords by piano.To specify the speed and rate of your song you have to put (use_bpm ###) at the top of your code.

Another amazing thing in sonic pi is its samples which make your song more interesting(I only have used of one of them) . You can alse give some effects to your code( with_fx ) such as reverb ,attack and ....

To make your code more clear and avoid writing repetitious lines you can use of Define(just like def in python) and call your function in different parts of your code but it's possible making bugs by using functions(It has happened for me so I was enjoined to comment my function😕)

At last I'm especially agrateful for Miss Mahya Khazaie and Mr Amouie which taught us some basics of music(chords ,musical notes etc)  and some of the syntaxes of this new language.
